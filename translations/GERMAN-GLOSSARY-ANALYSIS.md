## üéØ GERMAN-SPECIFIC CONSIDERATIONS

### Compound Words
- German loves compound words - use them naturally
- "Arbeitsblatt-Ersteller" not "Arbeitsblatt Ersteller"
- Hyphenate when necessary for clarity

### Sie-Form (Formal Address)
- ALWAYS use Sie-form for user instructions
- "Bitte erstellen Sie..." not "Bitte erstelle..."
- This is non-negotiable for professional educational software

### DIN Standards
- Always prefix A4 with "DIN" - Germans expect this
- DIN A4 is the German standard, not just A4

### Educational Context
- Prefer education-specific terms over technical terms
- "Aufgabe" (exercise) over "Problem"
- "Ergebnis" (result) over technical terms
