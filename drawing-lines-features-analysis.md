## App Description
Drawing Lines is a worksheet generator that creates line-tracing activities. Children draw lines to connect matching image pairs on the left and right sides of the page. This develops fine motor skills, hand-eye coordination, and pre-writing abilities.

## Templates Available (8 total)

### Landscape Templates (4 pairs each)
- **Curve 1** - Curved paths connecting left/right images
- **Curve 2** - Alternative curved pattern
- **Curve 3** - Third curved variation
- **Curve 4** - Fourth curved variation

### Portrait Templates
- **Diagonal 1** - 5 pairs, diagonal paths
- **Diagonal 2** - 5 pairs, alternative diagonal pattern
- **Horizontal 1** - 5 pairs, horizontal paths
- **Vertical 1** - 4 pairs, vertical paths
