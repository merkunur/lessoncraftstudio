Introduction: First Grade Cognitive Leap

üìä Age Range: 6-7 Years Old (1st Grade)

Major Developmental Shift from Kindergarten:

  • Reading: Sight words ‚Üí Decoding (sounding out unfamiliar words)
  • Math: Addition within 10 ‚Üí Addition within 20 (bridging through 10)
  • Writing: Letters ‚Üí Words ‚Üí Simple sentences
  • Working memory: 5-6 chunks ‚Üí 6-7 chunks
  • Attention span: 15 minutes ‚Üí 20-25 minutes

‚úÖ What 1st Graders CAN Do (New Capabilities)

  • Decode CVC words (cat, dog, run) + CVCe words (cake, bike, hope)
  • Read simple sentences (5-8 words)
  • Write 2-3 sentence stories
  • Add/subtract within 20 (with some using mental math)
  • Tell time to hour/half-hour
  • Understand beginning, middle, end (narrative structure)

‚ùå What They Still Struggle With

  • Spelling accuracy (inventive spelling common: "becuz" for "because")
  • Abstract concepts (fractions, multiplication)
  • Complex multi-step logic (>8 steps)
  • Reading fluency (slow, deliberate decoding)
