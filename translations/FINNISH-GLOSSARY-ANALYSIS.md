## üéØ FINNISH-SPECIFIC CONSIDERATIONS

### Finno-Ugric Language (NOT Indo-European!)
- Completely different structure from Germanic/Romance languages
- Agglutinative: words grow by adding suffixes
- No articles: "the" and "a" don't exist
- No grammatical gender
- No future tense (context indicates future)

### 15 Grammatical Cases
Examples in current translations:
- **Nominative**: "kuva" (image)
- **Genitive**: "sivun" (of the page)
- **Partitive**: "teksti√§" (some text), "kuvia" (some images)

- **Allative**: "vasemmalle" (to the left), "oikealle" (to the right)
