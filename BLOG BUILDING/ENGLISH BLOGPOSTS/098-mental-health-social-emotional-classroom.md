## Introduction: Addressing Student Mental Health

**Rising concern**: Student mental health challenges post-pandemic

**Statistics** (CDC, 2022):
```
Students reporting:
- Persistent sadness: 42%
- Anxiety: 37%
- Poor mental health affecting schoolwork: 22%

Teacher observation:
- Emotional outbursts increased
- Focus/attention decreased
- Motivation challenges common
```

**Academic impact**: Can't learn if emotionally dysregulated
```
Student experiencing anxiety:
- Fight-or-flight response activated
- Prefrontal cortex (learning center) offline
- Can't focus on math lesson (survival mode active)

Result: Mental health directly impacts academic performance
```

**Research** (Durlak et al., 2011): SEL programs improve achievement **11 percentile points** AND reduce emotional distress

**Key principle**: Address emotional needs to unlock academic potential (whole-child approach)

## Mindfulness Activities

**Present-moment awareness**

### Mindful Coloring

**Structured activity**:
```
Instructions on worksheet:

Mindful Mandala Coloring

Before you start:
1. Sit comfortably
2. Take 3 deep breaths
3. Clear your mind

While coloring:
- Focus only on coloring (not other thoughts)
- Notice the colors you choose
- Pay attention to the movement of your hand
- If your mind wanders, gently bring focus back to coloring

Duration: 10-15 minutes

Benefits:
- Reduces stress (meditative activity)
- Improves focus (mindfulness practice)
- No right/wrong (low-pressure)

Research: 10 min mindful coloring reduces anxiety 12% (Curry & Kasser, 2005)
```
