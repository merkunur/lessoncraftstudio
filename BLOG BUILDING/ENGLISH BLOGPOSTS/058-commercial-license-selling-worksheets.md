## Marketplace Comparison

### Teachers Pay Teachers

**Pros**:
✅ Teacher-focused (professional buyers, higher prices)
✅ Built-in traffic (teachers search TPT first)
✅ Answer key expectation (generators provide automatically)

**Cons**:
❌ Commission: 55% (TPT takes 45%) or 80% (if you pay for premium account)
❌ Competitive (many sellers)

**Best for**: Classroom-ready bundles, grade-level specific, standards-aligned

---

### Etsy

**Pros**:
✅ Parent market (different audience than TPT, less competition)
✅ Lower commission (6.5% transaction fee + 3% payment processing)
✅ Broader audience (not just US teachers)

**Cons**:
❌ Less teacher-specific traffic
❌ Lower price points

**Best for**: Homeschool resources, parent activities, family printables

---

### Your Own Website (Advanced)

**Pros**:
✅ Zero commission (keep 100% of sales)
✅ Build email list (repeat customers)
✅ Brand control

**Cons**:
❌ Must drive own traffic (SEO, ads, social media)
❌ Payment processing fees still apply (Stripe, PayPal)

**Recommendation**: Start on TPT + Etsy (built-in traffic), expand to website later
