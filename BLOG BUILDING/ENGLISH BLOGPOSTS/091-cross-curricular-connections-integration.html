Math + Reading Integration

Natural connections exist between mathematics and literacy through word problems, data interpretation, and measurement activities. Here's how to leverage them:

üìö Activity: Students Write Math Story Problems

Student-created problems:

1. "Wilbur weighs 50 pounds in September and 125 pounds in December.

  • Creative thinking (connect literature to numbers)

Science + Math Integration

STEM connection: Science relies on mathematical analysis. Every experiment generates data that requires mathematical interpretation.

Scientific Data Tables

‚úàÔ∏è Activity: Paper Airplane Engineering

Experiment: Which paper airplane design flies farthest?

Data collection:

Design B: 22 ft, 25 ft, 20 ft (average: 22 ft)

Worksheet questions (generated from data):

Math skills: Measurement, averages, range, prediction
