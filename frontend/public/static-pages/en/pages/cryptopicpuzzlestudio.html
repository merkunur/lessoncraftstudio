Design Your Image Cryptograms in Easy Steps!

1. Write Your Secret Message!

Enter puzzle title, instructions, and phrases for the cryptogram (up to 6 lines).

2. Create Your Picture Code!

Assign unique image to each letter. Use 'Auto-assign' with a theme, or manually pick images.

3. Set Clues & Generate!

Decide how many letters will be revealed as text clues. Then click 'Generate Puzzle'!

4. Download Your Coded Creation!

Download worksheet and answer key (decoded phrases & full image-letter key) as PDF.
