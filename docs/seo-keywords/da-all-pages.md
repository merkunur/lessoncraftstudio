- 33 product pages

## Anti-Cannibalization Rules

| Page Type | Reserved Primary Keyword Pattern | Danish Example |

| **Product pages** | `{app} generator` | addition opgave generator |
| **Theme hubs** | `{tema}opgaver til børn` | dyreopgaver til børn |
