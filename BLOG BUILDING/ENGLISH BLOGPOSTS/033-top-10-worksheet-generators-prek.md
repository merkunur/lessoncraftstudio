## Introduction: Developmentally-Appropriate PreK Worksheets

**Age range**: 3-5 years old (Pre-Kindergarten)

**Developmental characteristics**:
- **Fine motor skills**: Emerging (palmer grasp ‚Üí tripod grasp transition)
- **Attention span**: 5-10 minutes maximum per activity
- **Cognitive stage**: Pre-operational (Piaget) - concrete thinking, visual learning dominant
- **Working memory**: 3-4 chunks (limited capacity)
- **Pre-literacy**: Letter recognition beginning, no reading expected

**What PreK students CANNOT do**:
- ‚ùå Read text clues (use images only)
- ‚ùå Sustain attention >15 minutes
- ‚ùå Use standard pencil grip effectively (many still use fisted grip)
- ‚ùå Count beyond 10 reliably
- ‚ùå Understand abstract symbols (√ó, √∑, <, >)

**What PreK students CAN do**:
- ‚úÖ Recognize simple images (apple, cat, sun)
- ‚úÖ Count 1-5 concrete objects
- ‚úÖ Identify basic shapes, colors
- ‚úÖ Match identical pictures
- ‚úÖ Follow visual patterns (AB, ABB)
- ‚úÖ Cut along simple lines (with practice)

**Critical requirement**: Worksheets must use **images only**, minimal text, large elements (1+ inch size)
