## MATCHING APP UNIQUE FEATURES (Critical for Content)

### 4 Different Matching Modes
1. **Image ↔ Beginning Letter:**
   - Matches images to their starting letter
   - Great for phonics practice
   - Example: Apple → A

2. **Image+Word ↔ Image+Word:**
   - Both columns show image with word label
   - Students match identical pairs
   - Reading recognition practice

3. **Image/Word ↔ Image/Word:**
   - Flexible mode with images OR words
   - Can mix images and text
   - Maximum versatility

4. **Image ↔ Custom Word:**
   - Match images to custom typed words
   - Vocabulary practice
   - Definitions, translations, descriptions

### Content Selection Flexibility
- **Random Theme & Images:** Completely automated
- **Random from Chosen Theme:** Theme consistency
- **Select Specific Images:** Full teacher control

### Educational Applications
- Phonics and letter recognition
- Vocabulary building
- Spanish-English translation practice
- Sight word matching
- Math fact matching (using custom words)
- Concept connections
