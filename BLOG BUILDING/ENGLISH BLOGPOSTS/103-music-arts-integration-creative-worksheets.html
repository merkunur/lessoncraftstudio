Activity: Mandala Coloring Worksheet

Worksheet: Intricate mandala design (printable)

Instructions:

Scientific Illustration

Science + detailed drawing:

Observation Drawing: Plant Life Cycle

Materials: Real plant (or photo), pencil, worksheet

Instructions:
1. Observe plant carefully (2 minutes)
2. Draw what you see (not what you think plants look like)
3. Label parts: roots, stem, leaves, flower
4. Add details: veins in leaves, texture of bark

Accuracy matters: Scientific drawings must be precise

Skills developed:
- Observation: Noticing small details
- Fine motor: Controlled pencil movement
- Science: Plant anatomy understanding
- Patience: Taking time to draw carefully

Extension: Draw plant at different life stages (seed ‚Üí sprout ‚Üí adult ‚Üí flower)

Benefit: Art makes science memorable (visual memory stronger than text)

Geometry & Art Connection

Math made beautiful

Tessellation Worksheets

Repeating patterns (no gaps, no overlaps):

What is a tessellation?

Pattern of shapes that covers a surface with no gaps or overlaps

Examples in Nature:

  • Honeycomb (hexagons)
  • Fish scales (ovals)
  • Turtle shell (irregular polygons)
Create your own tessellation:

Step 1: Choose a shape (triangle, square, hexagon work best)
Step 2: Transform the shape (cut from one side, tape to opposite)
Step 3: Trace repeatedly to create pattern
Step 4: Color to enhance design

Worksheet: Grid paper provided, trace and color

Math concepts:
- Transformations (rotation, translation, reflection)
- Symmetry
- Geometric shapes

Artist connection: M.C. Escher (famous tessellation artist)

Symmetry Art

Mirror images:

Line Symmetry Drawing

Worksheet: Half-image provided (butterfly wing, face, tree)

Task: Complete the other half (mirror image)

Challenge levels:
Easy: Simple shapes, clear grid
Medium: Detailed image, small grid
Hard: Complex curves, no grid

Math skill: Understanding symmetry (line of reflection)
Art skill: Precise drawing, attention to detail

Extension: Find examples of symmetry in nature
(photograph and label line of symmetry)
