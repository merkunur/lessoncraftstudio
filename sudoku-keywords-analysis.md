## NOTES FOR CONTENT WRITING

### Natural Integration Tips

1. **Combine Multiple Keywords in One H3:**
   - "Free Printable Kindergarten Worksheets and First Grade Math Worksheets"
   - This counts as 3 keyword occurrences: "free printable worksheets", "kindergarten worksheets", "first grade worksheets"

2. **Rotate Keywords Across Sections:**
   - Don't use same keyword in every H3
   - Distribute across all 7 sections
   - Section 7 absorbs keywords that don't fit elsewhere

3. **Keep Sentences Short (15 words max):**
   - "Create kindergarten worksheets in 3 clicks."
   - "Download free printable worksheets as PDF."
   - Short sentences = better SEO readability score

4. **Keywords in First and Last Paragraphs:**
   - First sentence MUST include primary keyword
   - Last paragraph MUST include primary keyword
   - Higher SEO weight for these positions
