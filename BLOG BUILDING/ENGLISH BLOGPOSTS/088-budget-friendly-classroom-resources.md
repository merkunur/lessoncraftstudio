## Reusable Materials Strategies

**Reduce paper consumption**

### Dry-Erase Pockets

**One-time investment**: $50 for 30 pockets
```
How it works:
1. Print worksheet (one copy)
2. Insert into dry-erase pocket
3. Student completes with dry-erase marker
4. Erase, reuse 100+ times

Example:
Morning math warm-up (daily 5-problem worksheet):
- Without pockets: Print 30 copies × 180 days = 5,400 pages
- With pockets: Print 30 copies once, reuse all year = 30 pages
Paper saved: 5,370 pages ($268.50 at $0.05/page)

ROI: $50 investment saves $268.50/year (5.4× return)
```

## Paper-Saving Strategies

**Reduce printing costs**

### Duplex Printing

**Simple change, big savings**:
```
Single-sided: 180 worksheets = 180 pages
Duplex (double-sided): 180 worksheets = 90 pages
Savings: 90 pages per student = 2,700 pages/class = $135/year

Settings: Printer preferences → Two-sided printing → "Short edge" (flip)
```

---

### Display + Notebook Paper

**Eliminate photocopying**:
```
Traditional: Print 30 copies of worksheet
Alternative: Project ONE worksheet on screen, students copy to notebook

Example:
Math problems: Display 20 problems on projector
Students: Write problems + answers in math notebook
Paper used: 0 sheets

When appropriate: Computation practice, vocabulary definitions, comprehension questions
When not appropriate: Complex diagrams, lengthy passages
```
