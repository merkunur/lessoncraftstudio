Introduction: Developmentally-Appropriate PreK Worksheets

üìä Age Range: 3-5 Years Old (Pre-Kindergarten)

Developmental characteristics of PreK students:

What PreK Students CANNOT Do

‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Limitations for Ages 3-5

  • ‚ùå Read text clues (use images only)

  • ‚ùå Use standard pencil grip effectively (many still use fisted grip)

‚úÖ Age-Appropriate Capabilities

  • ‚úÖ Recognize simple images (apple, cat, sun)
  • ‚úÖ Count 1-5 concrete objects
  • ‚úÖ Identify basic shapes and colors
  • ‚úÖ Match identical pictures
  • ‚úÖ Follow visual patterns (AB, ABB)
  • ‚úÖ Cut along simple lines (with practice)

üéØ Critical Requirement

Worksheets must use images only, minimal text, and large elements (1+ inch size) to be developmentally appropriate for ages 3-5.
